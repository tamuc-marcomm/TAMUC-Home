We are proven

Texas A&M University-Commerce has been demonstrating our commitment to student success since 1889. As part of the A&M family of universities, we make the benefits of a big name system conveniently accessible. Our world-class faculty and state-of-the-art facilities combine to continue our tradition of empowering student accomplishment. We have a 126+ year record of constant improvement.

We are a vibrant academic community

We believe that our students and faculty comprise one team toward success. With residence halls, more than 120 clubs and organizations, an engaging Fraternity and Sorority community, a variety of intramural sports, and more than 1000 extracurricular activities, campus life develops the whole student. With fourteen collegiate sports teams competing in NCAA Division II earning 58 Lone Star Conference championships and 5 national championships, Lion pride brings students together. At a university with a diverse, yet drawn together student body and a faculty that will know your name, you will graduate with friends and memories as well as your degree.
